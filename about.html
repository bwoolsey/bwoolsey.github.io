About

Writer, technologist, lifelong learner

Hello, I'm Blake. This website is a place for me to collect ideas, projects, observations, and things I'm learning along the way. I enjoy exploring technology, solving problems, and documenting the occasional lesson worth remembering. Rather than chasing trends or publishing on a strict schedule, I prefer to write when I have something useful or interesting to share.

My interests span software, systems, history, personal projects, and the many ways technology intersects with everyday life. I am particularly drawn to subjects that reward curiosity and long-term thinking. Whether I'm working through a technical challenge or researching a topic that caught my attention, I enjoy the process of digging deeper and making sense of complex ideas.

This could be a collection of notes, articles, and personal projects. Some posts may focus on technical topics, while others may cover books, experiences, creative work, or subjects that simply seem worth exploring. My goal is not to be comprehensive, but to create a useful record of things I've learned and found valuable.

From time to time, you'll also find experiments, project write-ups, and reflections on challenges encountered along the way. I believe some of the best learning happens through building, testing, and occasionally making mistakes, and documenting that process can be just as useful as the final result.

I hope it becomes both a personal archive and a place where visitors can discover something useful, interesting, or thought provoking. Whether you've arrived through a shared interest, a search engine, or simple curiosity, I appreciate you taking the time to stop by.

Thanks for reading, and feel free to explore the rest of the site.
